Our Story

Built to close the gap between audience and income.

RAY was founded on a simple observation: the creators with the most genuine expertise and trust are often the worst monetized. That asymmetry shouldn't exist.

About RAY

We saw the inefficiency.
We built the fix.

RAY started with one observation: creators with deep expertise and loyal communities were still trading their time for brand deals — a monetization model that enriches platforms, not creators.

The problem was never effort or expertise. It was the absence of a business execution layer. Building a digital product requires product design, offer architecture, landing pages, payments infrastructure, launch strategy, and customer support — simultaneously. Most creators can't run all of that while also creating content.

So we built a team that does exactly that — and structured it as a partnership, not a service. We only succeed when you do. That keeps our incentives honest.

"Our mission is to help expert creators convert what they know into what they own."

FAQ

Honest answers
to real questions.

Is this really free to start? +
Yes — completely. We do not charge upfront. We take a percentage of revenue generated, typically 10–30% depending on the project scope, for a fixed period. After that, you keep 100% of everything.
What kind of digital product will we build? +
It depends on your niche and audience. Common formats include ebooks, mini-courses, toolkits, templates, and private community access. We decide together based on what your audience already wants from you — extracted from your DMs, comments, and existing content.
How long does the whole process take? +
From onboarding to launch, we target 30 days. Product creation takes 7–15 days. The remaining time is launch preparation, landing page setup, and payment infrastructure. First buyers typically arrive within 24–48 hours of launch.
How much of my time does this require? +
We handle the heavy lifting. You'll need 2–3 sessions with us totaling roughly 2–3 hours to share your expertise and sign off on the product direction. Beyond that, your main role is posting the launch content to your audience — you stay the creator.
What if the launch doesn't perform well? +
We don't earn if you don't earn — so our incentive is to make it work. If initial results are below expectations, we'll iterate on the product, offer, or launch approach with you. We're in it until the product works.
Will I own everything at the end? +
Yes. The product, the systems, the payment infrastructure, and the customer list — all of it transfers to you once the partnership period ends. From that point, every new follower you gain has direct economic value.
What platforms and tools do you use? +
We use Framer or Webflow for landing pages, Whop for payments and revenue splitting, and AI-powered workflows for product creation and customer support automation. Everything is handed over to you fully set up.
